Springhill Parents' Association

Welcome to Springhill Parents and Friends Association

"All parents and carers are members of the Parents and Friends Association"

We are dedicated to advancing the education and improving well-being of students at our school.

Our mission focuses on:

Building Effective Relationships: Developing strong partnerships between staff, parents, and the local community of the school.

Supporting Educational Activities: Hosting events to raise funds and provide resources to enhance the school's educational environment.

Join us in our efforts to create a supportive network that empowers every child with the opportunities to excel. Together, we can make a significant impact.

Current Vacancies

If you have the skills and can offer a few hours of your time a month to volunteer for one of the below posts, get in touch at springhillpa@gmail.co.uk

Secretary:

You:

  • Attend core PA meetings to understand upcoming events, key dates and what is needed from volunteers.
  • Take minutes at PA meetings and distribute them to the parent body.
  • Support the Chair and Deputy Chair in creating PA meeting Agendas.

Marketing Officer:

You:

  • Attend core PA meetings to understand upcoming events, key dates and what is needed from volunteers.
  • Communicate this key information in an engaging way via the PA social media tools and PA newsletter, to encourage volunteers and parent body participation.
  • Use your creative flair to generate eye-catching event posters that pull in volunteers from the parent body.
